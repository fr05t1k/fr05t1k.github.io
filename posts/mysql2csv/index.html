MySQL client out as CSV

2019-06-28

If you need to convert mysql-client output as CSV here is a recipe

alias mysql2csv='sed '\''s/\,/\\,/g;s/\"/\\"/g;s/\t/","/g;s/^/"/;s/$/"/;s/\n//g'\'''
mytsql -e "select * from users" | mysql2csv
